Wash Karo

Welcome To Wash Karo

Wash Karo is a free Android-based app which aims to spread awareness among the mass regarding Water and sanitation practices. The App provides the latest news articles and matching WHO reports in the domain of WASH (Water, Sanitation and Hygiene).

OUR APP FEATURES

Wash Karo is a research project based in TavLab under the guidance of Dr Tavpritesh Sethi which aims to educate and aware the less-educated people in our society by providing them free resource material related to Hygienic water practises in the local language(Hindi) without any cost. The app gives the latest news regarding Water and sanitation issues in India and provides a suitable WHO article to tackle the issue to its users.

FAQ

What is Wash Karo?

Wash Karo is a research project based in TavLab under the guidance of Dr Tavpritesh Sethi which aims to educate and aware the less-educated people in our society by providing them free resource material related to Hygienic water practises in the local language(Hindi) without any cost. The app gives the latest news regarding Water and sanitation issues in India and provides a suitable WHO article to tackle the issue to its users. The user can select any News articles from the list to read/listen to it and can check the related WHO article as well.
